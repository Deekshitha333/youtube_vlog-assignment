One of the more memorable scenes in Chaplin’s 1928 classic The Circus occurs when the Tramp,
chased by a mule, accidentally locks himself in a lion’s cage. Chaplin’s co-star in this
scene was “Numa,” a famous screen lion who had already appeared in several other films,
including The Extra Girl with Mabel Normand and The Missing Link with Charlie’s half-brother
Syd Chaplin.
